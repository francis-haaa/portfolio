About Me

The storyteller
behind the strategy

Your Photo Here

"I've always been a storyteller."

It started when I was a kid, listening to my grandfather's stories, imagining them as vivid visual narratives. That love for storytelling evolved into content creation — first through my own Instagram, which became my digital portfolio, and then professionally, helping brands tell their stories online.

Today, I combine creativity with analytics, crafting social media strategies that don't just look great but also drive engagement and sales. Whether it's a personal brand or a company, I help bring stories to life in a way that connects, resonates, and delivers real results.
